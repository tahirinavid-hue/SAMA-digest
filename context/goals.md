# SAMA Online Presence — Goals & Priorities

## Primary Goals
1. **Engage local Muslims** — reach Muslims in Grand Island, WNY, and nearby areas
2. **Drive attendance** — encourage visits to the musallah at 1822 Huth Rd
3. **Grow donations** — direct traffic to Zeffy (sadaqa) and LaunchGood (building fund)
4. **Raise awareness** — improve SAMA's visibility in the broader Grand Island community

## Success Indicators
- Increased musallah attendance (especially Jumu'ah and events)
- Growth in Instagram followers and engagement
- Donation volume growth on Zeffy and LaunchGood
- More local Muslims connected via WhatsApp/Facebook
- Community awareness of SAMA among non-Muslims in Grand Island

## Content Pillars
1. **Prayer & Worship** — prayer times, Jumu'ah reminders, Ramadan/Eid
2. **Programs & Events** — school enrollment, mentorship, community gatherings
3. **Community Service** — local charity, interfaith collaboration
4. **Announcements** — updates, new programs, building fund progress
5. **Inspiration** — Islamic reminders, community stories

## Key Audiences
- Muslims already in Grand Island / WNY (underserved, may not know SAMA exists)
- Muslim families looking for Islamic education for children
- Muslim young professionals and youth
- Broader Grand Island community (awareness + interfaith goodwill)
