1. 🎵 Tuesday Summer Concert at Town Common Tuesday, July 14 · 6:30 PM · Marion Klingel Town Common, 2255 Baseline Rd The Grand Island Recreation Department's free weekly outdoor concert series rolls on at Town Common. Grab a lawn chair and settle in for a free evening of live music — the Zonta Club runs a snack stand and 50/50 raffle alongside the show.

2. 📚 Library Book Club Tuesday, July 14 · 6:00 PM · Grand Island Memorial Library The evening book club meets on the second Tuesday of each month and is open to all. This month's pick is The House on Mango Street by Sandra Cisneros — physical copies are available to borrow from the library.

3. 🎵 Tuesday Summer Concert at Town Common Tuesday, July 21 · 6:30 PM · Marion Klingel Town Common, 2255 Baseline Rd Another week, another free outdoor concert at Town Common courtesy of the Recreation Department. The full series runs every Tuesday through August 11, with the Zonta Club on hand for snacks and a 50/50 each week.
