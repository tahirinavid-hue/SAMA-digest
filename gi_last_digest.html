📅 Week 2: Jun 15 – Jun 21

1. 🏛️ Town Board Workshop Mon, Jun 15 · 6:30 PM – 8:00 PM · First Floor Conference Room, Town Hall, 2255 Baseline Rd

The monthly Town Board Workshop where local officials review agenda items and policy matters ahead of the regular meeting. Open to the public and streamed live.

2. 🏛️ Town Board Meeting Mon, Jun 15 · 8:00 PM – 10:00 PM · Court Room, Town Hall, 2255 Baseline Rd

The regular monthly Town Board meeting is open to in-person public attendance and also streamed live.

3. 📚 Book a Technology Trainer Mon, Jun 15 · 11:00 AM – 5:00 PM · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd

One-on-one technology help sessions are available on June 15th between 11:00 AM and 5:00 PM at the library.

These drop-in style slots are a good resource if you have questions about devices, email, or using the internet. Call 716-773-7124 to book your slot.

4. 🏛️ Board of Architectural Review Tue, Jun 16 · 7:30 PM – 9:00 PM · Grand Island, NY 14072

The town's architectural review board meets to evaluate proposed building and design projects on the island. Meetings are public.

5. 🏛️ Historic Preservation Board Wed, Jun 17 · 5:00 PM – 7:00 PM · Grand Island, NY 14072

The Historic Preservation Board holds its regular monthly meeting. Members of the public with an interest in local history and preservation are welcome to attend.

6. 🏛️ Agricultural Advisory Board Thu, Jun 18 · 10:00 AM – 12:00 PM · First Floor Conference Room, Town Hall, 2255 Baseline Rd

The Agricultural Advisory Board meets to discuss farming and land use matters affecting the island. Open to the public.

7. 🏛️ Technology Advisory Board Thu, Jun 18 · 7:00 PM – 9:00 PM · Grand Island, NY 14072

The Technology Advisory Board holds its monthly session to advise the town on tech infrastructure and digital services. Public is welcome to observe.

A note for families: The Grand Island Memorial Library's big summer kickoff events — including the Foam Fest dance party and Ice Cream Social for all ages — are confirmed for Monday, July 6.

Registration for the Foam Fest opens Monday, June 8

, so now is the time to get your kids signed up before spots fill. Call 716-773-7124 or stop in at the library.
