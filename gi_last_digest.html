📅 Week 1: May 18 – May 24

1. 👨‍👩‍👧 Toddler Story Time Saturday, May 23 · 10:00–11:00 AM · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd

This recurring Saturday program is tailored for little ones who are mobile and ready to explore the world — roughly 18 months to 3 or 4 years old, with a parent or caregiver. Activities weave together early literacy with reading, music, movement, singing, and learn-through-play toys and puzzles.

Registration required; call 716-773-7124.

2. 📚 Electric Vehicles Part 2: Home Chargers Saturday, May 23 · 1:00–3:00 PM · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd

Retired engineer Bill Brandoni returns with the second installment of his EV series, this time focusing on home chargers — covering his research before installing one, his experiences since, best charger picks, installation tips, and problems and solutions.

Registration is required; call 716-773-7124.

3. 📚 One-on-One Tech Trainer Saturday, May 24 · 11:00 AM–5:00 PM · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd

Grab a 45-minute private session with a tech trainer on topics ranging from Android and iPhone basics to Microsoft Office, cloud services, social media, Windows basics, and more.

Register for a slot by calling 716-773-7124.

📅 Week 2: May 25 – May 31

4. 🏛️ Memorial Day Ceremony Monday, May 25 · 10:00 AM · Charles N. DeGlopper Memorial Park (intersection of Grand Island Blvd & Baseline Rd)

The Town of Grand Island, VFW Post 9249, American Legion NY Post 1346, and the Grand Island High School Wind Ensemble will honor the men and women who made the supreme sacrifice for the country while in military service.

The whole community is welcome at the 10 a.m. program, and a reception follows at VFW Post 9249, located at 2121 Grand Island Boulevard.

5. 👨‍👩‍👧 Sprouts with Ms. Melissa Tuesday, May 26 · 10:00 AM · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd

Ms. Melissa leads this music-based program designed to help little ones learn and grow — open to ages 0 to 5 with a caregiver.

Registration is required; stop in or call 716-773-7124 to sign up.

6. 📚 Technology Buying Guide Class Wednesday, May 29 · 5:30–7:30 PM · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd

Thinking about buying a new tablet, laptop, computer, or smart TV but not sure where to start? This class covers what to know before buying and the tech specs that actually matter.

Registration required; call 716-773-7124.

7. 🎨 Watercolor Iris Painting Thursday, May 30 · 6:00 PM · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd

Open to adults ages 18 and up, this session led by instructor Adele Upton walks participants through painting an iris flower in watercolor — suitable for cards, bookmarks, or framing, with no prior painting experience needed and all materials provided.

Registration is required; call 716-773-7124.
