1. 👨‍👩‍👧 Toddler Story Time Saturday, May 23 · 10:00–11:00 AM · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd Designed for little ones roughly 18 months to 4 years old with a parent or caregiver. Activities mix early literacy with music, movement, storytelling, and hands-on play. Registration is required; call 716-773-7124.

2. 📚 Electric Vehicles Part 2: Home Chargers Saturday, May 23 · 1:00pm–3:00pm · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd Retired engineer Bill Brandoni returns with the second installment of his EV series, covering his research into home chargers, the installation process, and lessons learned along the way. Questions and comments are welcome.

Registration is required; call 716-773-7124.

3. 📚 One-on-One Tech Training Saturday, May 24 · 11:00am–5:00pm · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd Drop in for a free 45-minute private session with a Tech Trainer covering topics like Android and iPhone basics, email, cloud services, Microsoft Office, social media, and more. Registration is required; call 716-773-7124 to book a slot.

4. 🏛️ Memorial Day Ceremony Monday, May 25 · 10:00am · Charles N. DeGlopper Memorial Park (intersection of Grand Island Blvd & Baseline Rd) Veterans, families, and the whole community are invited to this annual Memorial Day observance at DeGlopper Memorial Park. A reception follows at VFW Post 9249, 2121 Grand Island Boulevard.

5. 👨‍👩‍👧 Sprouts with Ms. Melissa Tuesday, May 26 · 10:00am · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd Ms. Melissa leads a fun music-based program designed to support learning and development in children ages 0–5 with a caregiver. Registration is required; stop in or call 716-773-7124.

6. 📚 Tech Buyer's Guide Class Friday, May 29 · 5:30pm–7:30pm · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd Thinking about picking up a new tablet, laptop, computer, or smart TV but not sure where to start? This class walks you through what to know before buying, including tech specs to look out for.

7. 🎨 Watercolor Iris Painting Workshop Saturday, May 30 · 6:00pm · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd Instructor Adele Upton leads adults (18+) through an easy watercolor technique to create an iris flower piece suitable for cards, bookmarks, or framing. No prior watercolor experience is needed and all materials are provided.

Registration is required; call 716-773-7124.
