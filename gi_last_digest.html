1. 👨‍👩‍👧 Toddler Story Time Saturday, May 23 · 10:00 AM – 11:00 AM · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd A morning program built for newly mobile little ones, ages 18 months to about 4 years old. Expect stories, music, movement, and some learn-through-play toys — caregiver required.

2. 📚 EV Home Chargers: Part 2 Saturday, May 23 · 1:00 PM – 3:00 PM · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd Retired engineer Bill Brandoni returns with the second installment of his electric vehicle series, this time focused on home charging. He'll cover the best chargers, installation tips, and lessons learned from his own experience. Registration required — call 716-773-7124.

3. 📚 One-on-One Tech Help at the Library Saturday, May 24 · 11:00 AM – 5:00 PM · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd Sign up for a free 45-minute session with a Tech Trainer covering topics like email, cloud services, iPhone/iPad basics, Microsoft Office, social media, and more. Registration required — call 716-773-7124.

4. 🏛️ Memorial Day Ceremony Monday, May 25 · 10:00 AM · Charles N. DeGlopper Memorial Park, Grand Island Blvd & Baseline Rd The Town of Grand Island, VFW Post 9249, American Legion NY Post 1346, and the Grand Island High School Wind Ensemble will gather to honor those who gave their lives in military service. The ceremony is followed by a reception at VFW Post 9249 at 2121 Grand Island Blvd. The morning begins earlier with rifle salutes and taps at island cemeteries starting at 7:40 AM.

5. 👨‍👩‍👧 Sprouts with Ms. Melissa Tuesday, May 26 · 10:00 AM · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd Ms. Melissa leads a music-based program for children ages 0 to 5, designed to support early learning and development through song and movement. Caregivers attend with children. Registration required — stop in or call 716-773-7124.

6. 📚 Tech Buying Tips Workshop Wednesday, May 29 · 5:30 PM – 7:30 PM · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd Thinking about buying a new tablet, laptop, smart TV, or other device but not sure where to start? This class covers what to know before purchasing, including key tech specs to look for. Registration required — call 716-773-7124.

7. 🎨 Watercolor Iris Painting Workshop Thursday, May 30 · 6:00 PM · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd Instructor Adele Upton leads an accessible watercolor class where participants create an iris flower painting suitable for cards, bookmarks, or framing. No prior experience needed — all materials provided. Ages 18 and up. Registration required — call 716-773-7124.
