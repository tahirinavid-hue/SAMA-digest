📅 Week 1: Jun 06 – Jun 07

1. 🙌 Rotary Club Document Destruction Day Saturday, Jun 6 · 10:00 AM – 1:00 PM · Sidway School Parking Lot

The Rotary Club of Grand Island is presenting its annual document destruction event, set for 10 a.m. to 1 p.m. Saturday, June 6, at a new location: Sidway School parking lot.

It's a drive-through, in-vehicle service where you can watch your sensitive papers get shredded on the spot, with proceeds supporting community causes.

2. 🙌 Relay for Life of Grand Island Saturday, Jun 6 · 3:00 PM – 10:30 PM · Town Commons, 1959 Whitehaven Road

Proceeds from the Lawnmower Race go to support Grand Island's Relay For Life, which takes place from 3 to 10:30 p.m. Saturday, June 6, at the Town Commons. The lawnmower event is named after Mary Dunbar-Daluisio, longtime Relay chair, who died in 2017. This year marks the 25th anniversary of Grand Island's Relay For Life.

Note that

Whitehaven Road between Baseline and Grand Island Blvd. will be closed Saturday for Relay for Life.

📅 Week 2: Jun 08 – Jun 14

3. 🏛️ GI Historical Society Installation Dinner Tuesday, Jun 9 · 6:00 PM cash bar, 6:30 PM dinner · 773 North, 2749 Grand Island Blvd.

The Grand Island Historical Society's Installation of Officers dinner is Tuesday, June 9, 2026. The group will be dining at 773 North, 2749 Grand Island Blvd., with a buffet dinner, cash bar at 6:00 PM, and dinner at 6:30 PM.

Reservations should be made by calling Carolyn Doebert at 716-773-2643 by June 3, 2026.

This is the society's annual gathering to seat incoming officers.

4. 📚 Grand Island Memorial Library Book Club Tuesday, Jun 9 · 6:00 PM · Grand Island Memorial Library

The Library Book Club meets monthly on 2nd Tuesdays at 6:00 PM. All are welcome, and physical copies of the books will be available to borrow.

It's a relaxed evening gathering open to any reader who wants to discuss the month's selection with neighbors.

5. 👨‍👩‍👧 Mary Dunbar-Daluisio Memorial Lawnmower Race & Family Day Sunday, Jun 14 · 9:00 AM – 3:00 PM · 3957 West River Parkway (median near Long Road)

There's a new patriotic theme for the annual Mary Dunbar-Daluisio Memorial Lawnmower Race — it's being held on Flag Day, Sunday, June 14. The event, a longtime Father's Day tradition, was switched to Flag Day this year to avoid a conflict with an air show, and organizers kept the new date even after that show was canceled.

The Lawnmower Race runs from 9 a.m. to 3 p.m. at 3957 West River Parkway, and mower categories range from stock mowers to machines capable of 70 mph in 300 feet. The day also includes the Kevin Doring Memorial 5K Walk/Run, a golf cart race, a kids' mower race with mowers provided, a car/bike/truck show, games, and food for purchase.

All proceeds benefit Grand Island's Relay For Life.
