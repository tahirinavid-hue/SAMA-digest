📅 Week 1: May 18 – May 24

1. 👨‍👩‍👧 Toddler Story Time Saturday, May 23 · 10:00 AM – 11:00 AM · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd

A weekly Saturday morning program designed for mobile little ones roughly 18 months to 3 or 4 years old. Caregivers join in as the session weaves together reading, storytelling, music, movement, and learn-through-play toys.

Registration is required and space is limited.

2. 📚 Electric Vehicles Part 2: Home Chargers Saturday, May 23 · 1:00 PM – 3:00 PM · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd

Retired engineer Bill Brandoni returns with the second installment of his EV series, sharing what he learned while researching home charger options before installing one, and what he has experienced since. Topics include best chargers, installation tips, and real-world problems and solutions.

Registration is required — call 716-773-7124.

📅 Week 2: May 25 – May 31

3. 🏛️ Memorial Day Cemetery Salutes Monday, May 25 · Beginning 7:40 AM · Various Grand Island Cemeteries

VFW Post 9249 and American Legion NY Post 1346 will make a series of early-morning stops at Island cemeteries to honor veterans with a rifle salute and taps. The schedule runs from 7:40 a.m. at Whitehaven Cemetery through stops at Maple Grove, Staley Family Cemetery, and Assumption Cemetery.

4. 🏛️ Memorial Day Ceremony Monday, May 25 · 10:00 AM · Charles N. DeGlopper Memorial Park, Grand Island Blvd & Baseline Rd

The Town of Grand Island, VFW Post 9249, American Legion NY Post 1346, and the Grand Island High School Wind Ensemble will hold a formal ceremony honoring those who gave their lives in military service. A reception follows at VFW Post 9249, 2121 Grand Island Boulevard.

5. 👨‍👩‍👧 Sprouts with Ms. Melissa Tuesday, May 26 · 10:00 AM · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd

A drop-in music and learning program for children ages 0 to 5 with a caregiver, led by Ms. Melissa. Registration is required — stop in or call 716-773-7124.

6. 📚 Tech Buying Guide: What to Know Before You Buy Wednesday, May 29 · 5:30 PM – 7:30 PM · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd

A class for anyone thinking about purchasing a new tablet, computer, laptop, or smart TV who isn't sure where to start. It covers what to look for before buying and which tech specifications actually matter.

7. 🎨 Watercolor Iris Painting Workshop Thursday, May 30 · 6:00 PM · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd

Registration is required — call 716-773-7124.
