1. 👨‍👩‍👧 Meet the Monarchs with Eastern Monarch Butterfly Farm Monday, July 20 · Noon · Grand Island Memorial Library, Bedell Rd

Hawk Creek — wait, this is Eastern Monarch Butterfly Farm visiting the library at noon on July 20. All ages are welcome to learn about monarch butterflies, butterfly gardens, migration patterns, and what people can do to help protect the species.

Registration is required; call 716-773-7124.

2. 🎵 Tuesday Summer Concert Tuesday, July 21 · 6:30 PM · Marion Klingel Town Common, 2255 Baseline Rd

The Grand Island Recreation Department's free outdoor concert series continues every Tuesday evening at Marion Klingel Town Common, with the whole family invited out for a night of live local music.

The Zonta Club of Grand Island runs the snack stand and a 50/50 raffle at each concert.

3. 📚 Treat Your Shelf Teen Book Club Thursday, July 23 · 1:00–3:00 PM · Grand Island Memorial Library, Bedell Rd

Teens entering grades 6–9 gather on Thursdays through August 6 for crafts, activities, and book trivia centered on "City of Spies" by James Ponti. Books are provided to all participants, and registration is required beginning June 16.

4. 🌿 Paddles Up Niagara — 20th Annual Saturday, July 25 · 7:00 AM–1:00 PM · Beaver Island State Park, Grand Island

Paddles Up Niagara is a family-friendly way to explore the natural areas and wildlife habitats of the Niagara River, welcoming everyone from first-timers to seasoned paddlers, with a safety team on the water to keep the group clear of motor boats.

Environmental exhibitors are on-site from 9:30 AM to 12:30 PM, sharing water safety information and resources about the Great Lakes and Niagara River.

Online registration closes at noon on July 23.

5. 🎵 Tuesday Summer Concert: Barroom Buzzards Tuesday, July 28 · 6:30 PM · Marion Klingel Town Common, 2255 Baseline Rd

The Barroom Buzzards bring their New Orleans jazz and Dixieland sound to Town Common for this week's free summer concert.

Lawn chairs and blankets are welcome.

6. 👨‍👩‍👧 Walk With Eagles Project — Hawk Creek Wildlife Center Monday, July 29 · 12:00–1:00 PM · Grand Island Memorial Library, Bedell Rd

Hawk Creek Wildlife Center brings two live eagle ambassadors to the library for an up-close look — no touching, since these are wild animals with sharp talons — along with a presentation, crafts, and a Q&A session. Photos are encouraged.

Registration is required and began June 29; call 716-773-7124.

7. 👨‍👩‍👧 Llama Llama in the Library Monday, August 3 · 12:00–1:00 PM · Grand Island Memorial Library, Bedell Rd

All ages are invited to meet two llamas from Llama Adventures, with a chance to touch and interact with the animals, learn about them, and take home a llama craft. Space is limited and registration is required beginning July 6; call 716-773-7124.

8. 🙌 National Night Out Tuesday, August 4 · 6:00 PM · Marion Klingel Town Common, 2255 Baseline Rd

National Night Out comes to Town Common at 6:00 PM, the annual community-building event held nationwide to strengthen neighborhood connections and support local public safety.

9. 🎵 Tuesday Summer Concert: Rock Angel Tuesday, August 4 · 6:30 PM · Marion Klingel Town Common, 2255 Baseline Rd

Rock Angel is a high-energy performance band that covers a wide range of styles — rock, pop, blues, southern rock, country, funk, and a touch of jazz — with a focus on a dynamic live show.

Free and open to all.

10. 📚 Library Book Club Evening Tuesday, August 11 · 6:00 PM · Grand Island Memorial Library, Bedell Rd

The evening book club meets on the second Tuesday of each month at 6:00 PM, with physical copies of the selection available to borrow. In August the group reads "The Heaven & Earth Grocery Store" by James McBride.

11. 🎵 Tuesday Summer Concert: Yank the Cover Tuesday, August 11 · 6:30 PM · Marion Klingel Town Common, 2255 Baseline Rd

Yank the Cover, billed as a premier WNY cover band, wraps up the 2026 summer concert series with an eclectic mix of classic rock, R&B, top 40, and country music.

This is the final concert of the season.
