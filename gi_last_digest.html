1. 🙌 Relay for Life of Grand Island

Saturday, Jun 6 · 3:00 PM – 10:30 PM · Town Commons, 1959 Whitehaven Rd

This year marks the 25th anniversary of Grand Island's Relay For Life. The lawnmower race earlier in the day is named after Mary Dunbar-Daluisio, longtime Relay chair, who died in 2017. Note: Whitehaven Road between Baseline and Grand Island Blvd. will be closed Saturday for the event.

2. 🏛️ GI Historical Society Installation Dinner

Tuesday, Jun 9 · Cash bar 6:00 PM, Dinner 6:30 PM · 773 North, 2749 Grand Island Blvd

The Grand Island Historical Society's annual installation of officers dinner, held at 773 North with a buffet dinner. Reservations required — call Carolyn Doebert at 716-773-2643 (deadline was Jun 3, so contact them directly to confirm availability).

3. 📚 Grand Island Memorial Library Book Club

Tuesday, Jun 9 · 6:00 PM · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd

The library's book club meets monthly on the second Tuesday at 6:00 PM. All are welcome, and physical copies of the month's selection are available to borrow. A relaxed evening open to any reader who wants to discuss the book with neighbors.

4. 👨‍👩‍👧 Mary Dunbar-Daluisio Memorial Lawnmower Race & Family Day

Sunday, Jun 14 · 9:00 AM – 3:00 PM · 3957 West River Pkwy (median near Long Rd)

The annual lawnmower race moves to Flag Day this year, running 9 AM–3 PM on West River Parkway. Mower categories range from stock to machines capable of 70 mph in 300 feet. The day also includes the Kevin Doring Memorial 5K Walk/Run, a golf cart race, a kids' mower race (mowers provided), a car/bike/truck show, games, and food. All proceeds benefit Grand Island's Relay For Life.
