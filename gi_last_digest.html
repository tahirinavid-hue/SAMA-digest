1. 🏃 Dick Bessel Independence Day Run & Kids Race Saturday, Jul 4 · 9:00 AM · Grand Island Blvd / Town Hall, 2255 Baseline Rd

All kids who finish the youth race get a medal at the finish line.

Day-of registration is available at Town Hall from 7:00–8:45 AM.

2. 👨‍👩‍👧 Independence Day Parade Saturday, Jul 4 · ~10:00 AM · Grand Island Blvd

Road closures run from 8:30 AM to noon, with Grand Island Boulevard closed from the traffic circle to the Baseline Road intersection.

3. 🌿 Nature Journal Walk at Beaver Island State Park Saturday, Jul 4 · Beaver Island State Park, Grand Island

A park naturalist leads a stroll through Little Beaver Island — a perfect outing for anyone wanting to swap fireworks prep for some quiet riverside nature time.

Registration is required through Eventbrite.com.

4. 👨‍👩‍👧 Summer Reading Kickoff: Ice Cream Social & Bubble Bash Monday, Jul 6 · 2:00 PM · Grand Island Memorial Library

Hosted by the Friends of the Grand Island Memorial Library, this free all-ages party features bubbles, crafts, lawn games, and ice cream.

Wear your bathing suit and flip flops and bring a towel — things are going to get wet.

5. 🎵 Tuesday Summer Concert: Rock Revival Tuesday, Jul 7 · 6:30 PM · Marion Klingel Town Common, 2255 Baseline Rd

Rock Revival brings classic rock, blues, and country to the outdoor stage — a four-piece group of veteran local players with decades of WNY performance behind them.

The weekly concert series is free and open to the whole family.

6. 👨‍👩‍👧 Campout in Your Pajamas Thursday, Jul 9 · 6:30–7:30 PM · Grand Island Memorial Library (Barb's Book Buddies camper, behind the library)

Kids are invited to pull on their pajamas, grab a stuffed animal, and settle in for an interactive bedtime story experience in a camper parked behind the library.

It's a cozy evening out the whole family can enjoy together.

7. 📚 Library Evening Book Club Tuesday, Jul 14 · 6:00 PM · Grand Island Memorial Library

The monthly evening book club meets on the second Tuesday of each month at 6:00 PM; July's title is The House on Mango Street by Sandra Cisneros.

All are welcome, and physical copies of the book are available to borrow from the library.

8. 📚 Treat Your Shelf Teen Book Club Thursday, Jul 17 · 1:00–3:00 PM · Grand Island Memorial Library

Open to teens entering grades 6–9, this Thursday afternoon club pairs crafts and book trivia with reading City of Spies by James Ponti — and the library purchases the book for each participant.

Registration is required and opened June 16.

9. 👨‍👩‍👧 Read to a Dog Friday, Jul 17 · 1:00–3:00 PM · Grand Island Memorial Library

Open to all ages, this program lets kids and readers of any level practice reading aloud to certified therapy dogs from the SPCA's Paws 4 Love Program — attentive listeners who never judge a stumble.

Sign up for a personal 20-minute session by calling 716-773-7124.
