📅 Week 1: May 18 – May 24

1. 👨‍👩‍👧 Toddler Story Time Saturday, May 23 · 10:00–11:00am · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd Designed for little ones who are on the move and ready to explore, this weekly session for roughly ages 18 months to 3 or 4 years features reading, music, movement, singing, and learn-through-play toys. Registration is required — call 716-773-7124.

2. 📚 Electric Vehicles Part 2: Home Chargers Saturday, May 23 · 1:00–3:00pm · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd Retired engineer Bill Brandoni returns with the second installment of his EV series, covering his research into home charging options, installation tips, and real-world experience since having one installed. All questions welcome. Registration required — call 716-773-7124.

📅 Week 2: May 25 – May 31

3. 🏛️ Memorial Day Ceremony Monday, May 25 · 10:00am · Charles N. DeGlopper Memorial Park (intersection of Grand Island Blvd & Baseline Rd) The Town of Grand Island, VFW Post 9249, American Legion NY Post 1346, and the Grand Island High School Wind Ensemble gather to honor those who served and gave their lives. A reception follows at VFW Post 9249 at 2121 Grand Island Blvd. Prior to the main ceremony, starting at 7:40am, posts will conduct a rifle salute and playing of taps at several island cemeteries including Whitehaven, Maple Grove, Assumption, Trinity, and St. Stephen's.

4. 👨‍👩‍👧 Sprouts with Ms. Melissa Tuesday, May 26 · 10:00am · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd Ms. Melissa leads this music-based session for little ones ages 0–5 with a caregiver, helping young children learn and grow through song and sound. Registration is required — stop in or call 716-773-7124.

5. 📚 Tech Help: Buying New Technology Wednesday, May 29 · 5:30–7:30pm · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd Thinking about a new tablet, laptop, computer, or smart TV but not sure where to start? This class covers what to know before you buy and which tech specs actually matter.

Register for a session by calling 716-773-7124.

6. 🎨 Watercolor Iris Painting Workshop Thursday, May 30 · 6:00pm · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd Instructor Adele Upton guides participants through an easy watercolor technique to paint a beautiful iris flower — suitable for cards, bookmarks, or framing. No prior watercolor experience needed, and all materials are provided. Ages 18 and up. Registration required — call 716-773-7124.
