1. 🏃 Dick Bessel Independence Day Run & Kids Race Saturday, Jul 4 · 9:00 AM · Grand Island Town Hall, 2255 Baseline Rd

The Dick Bessel Independence Day Run takes place Saturday, July 4, 2026, and includes both a 2.3-mile run/walk for all ages and a separate Kids Race for ages 4–10.

The adult race starts at 9:10 AM and the Kids Race kicks off at 9:00 AM.

Every kid who crosses the finish line gets a medal.

2. 🎉 Independence Day Parade Saturday, Jul 4 · 10:00 AM · Grand Island Boulevard

The Independence Day Parade starts at 10:00 AM and runs along Grand Island Boulevard.

It follows right on the heels of the Dick Bessel Run — a great reason to stick around after the race with a lawn chair and enjoy the show.

3. 👨‍👩‍👧 Ice Cream Social & Bubble Bash Monday, Jul 6 · 2:00 PM · Grand Island Memorial Library

This Summer Reading Kickoff Party, hosted by the Friends of the Grand Island Memorial Library, starts at 2 PM on Monday, July 6, with no registration required, while supplies last, and all ages are welcome — featuring bubbles, crafts, activities, lawn games, and ice cream.

Bathing suits, flip flops, and a towel are recommended.

4. 🎵 Tuesday Summer Concert: Rock Revival Tuesday, Jul 7 · 6:30 PM · Marion Klingel Town Common, 2255 Baseline Rd

Rock Revival takes the stage July 7 at 6:30 PM, playing a mix of classic rock, blues, and today's country, with four seasoned local players who have been performing in the WNY area for decades.

The concert is free, brought to you by the Grand Island Recreation Department.

5. 👨‍👩‍👧 Campout in Your Pajamas Thursday, Jul 9 · 6:30–7:30 PM · Grand Island Memorial Library (Barb's Book Buddies camper)

On Thursday, July 9 from 6:30 to 7:30 PM, kids are invited to put on their pajamas, grab a favorite stuffed animal, and take part in an interactive family reading experience, gathering at Barb's Book Buddies camper behind the library for bedtime stories.

A cozy, low-key evening program that's great for the whole family.

6. 📚 Library Evening Book Club Tuesday, Jul 14 · 6:00 PM · Grand Island Memorial Library

The Library Book Club meets monthly on the second Tuesday of each month at 6:00 PM, and all are welcome.

The July 14 pick is The House on Mango Street by Sandra Cisneros.

Physical copies are available to borrow from the library.

7. 📚 Joseph Ellicott: Why Is His Name Found in Every Corner of WNY? Wednesday, Jul 22 · 6:30–7:30 PM · Grand Island Memorial Library

This library program on July 22 features Island resident and reenactor Dennis Upton discussing the extraordinary life of Joseph Ellicott, the founding father of Buffalo, Batavia, and much of Western New York.

A fun history talk that's accessible for curious adults and older kids alike.

8. 🌿 Paddles Up Niagara (20th Annual) Saturday, Jul 25 · 7:00 AM–1:00 PM · Beaver Island State Park (Check in at Shelter 2B)

Paddles Up Niagara is a family-friendly event offering a safe way to explore the natural areas and unique habitats of the Niagara River, welcoming everyone from first-time paddlers to experienced ones.

Guides and safety paddlers are on hand throughout the morning, and after the paddle you can visit exhibitors and vendors, then stick around for refreshments and free prize drawings.

Registration for July 25 is open through July 23.
