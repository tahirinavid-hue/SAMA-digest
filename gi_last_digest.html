1. 📚 Library Evening Book Club

Tuesday, Jun 02 · 6:00 PM · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd

The library's monthly evening book club meets on the second Tuesday of each month. June's title is The Guncle by Stephen Rowley. All are welcome, and physical copies are available to borrow.

2. 🎉 Medieval Times Dungeon Birthday Party

Wednesday, Jun 04 · 11:30 AM · Grand Island Golden Age Center

The Golden Age Center is celebrating June birthdays with a medieval-themed lunch party. Guests are encouraged to wear period dress, take part in axe throwing and boulder toss games, eat without utensils, and enjoy live music from Nan Hoffman after lunch.

3. 🌿 Bird Identification with Reinstein Woods

Thursday, Jun 05 · 10:00 AM · Golden Age Center, Front Dining Area

A naturalist from Reinstein Woods will lead a presentation on identifying local birds — songs, plumage, and how to use binoculars and field guides. Call the Golden Age Center at (716) 773-9682 to sign up.

4. 🙌 Relay For Life of Grand Island — 25th Anniversary

Saturday, Jun 06 · relayforlife.org/GrandIslandNY

This year's Relay marks 25 consecutive years of this community cancer fundraiser on the island. The day kicks off with the Kevin Doring Memorial Walk at 10:00 AM, followed by a car, truck, and bike show, lawnmower races (including a free kids' race), face painting, a photo booth, and food from the Grand Moose Lodge.
