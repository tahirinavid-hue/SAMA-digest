📅 Week 1: May 18 – May 24

1. 👨‍👩‍👧 Toddler Story Time

Saturday, May 23 · 10:00am – 11:00am · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd This weekly Saturday session is geared toward newly mobile little ones, roughly 18 months to 3 or 4 years old, with a parent or caretaker. Expect early literacy games, music, movement, and learn-through-play toys. Registration required; call 716-773-7124.

2. 📚 Electric Vehicles Part 2: Home Chargers

Saturday, May 23 · 1:00pm – 3:00pm · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd Retired engineer Bill Brandoni returns for the second installment of his EV series, sharing his hands-on research into home charger options, installation tips, and lessons learned since having one installed. All questions welcome. Registration required; call 716-773-7124.

3. 📚 One-on-One Tech Trainer Sessions

Saturday, May 24 · 11:00am – 5:00pm · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd Drop in for a private 45-minute session with a tech trainer covering topics like Android and iPhone basics, Microsoft Office, cloud services, social media, and more. Free for library patrons 17 and up. Registration required; call 716-773-7124.

📅 Week 2: May 25 – May 31

4. 🏛️ Memorial Day Ceremony

Monday, May 25 · 10:00am · Charles N. DeGlopper Memorial Park, intersection of Grand Island Blvd & Baseline Rd The community, veterans, and families are invited to attend the annual Memorial Day program at DeGlopper Memorial Park. A reception follows the ceremony at VFW Post 9249 Charles N. DeGlopper Memorial Post, 2121 Grand Island Boulevard.

5. 👨‍👩‍👧 Sprouts with Ms. Melissa

Tuesday, May 26 · 10:00am · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd Ms. Melissa leads this music-based learning session for babies and toddlers ages 0 to 5 with a caregiver, helping young children learn and grow through song and movement. Registration required; stop in or call 716-773-7124.

6. 🏃 1st Annual Chamber Corporate Challenge

Thursday, May 28 · Grand Island The Grand Island Chamber of Commerce is hosting its first-ever Corporate Challenge, a team competition open to local businesses. Only 18 team spots are available, so registration is encouraged soon through the Chamber's website.

7. 📚 Tech Buying Guide Workshop

Friday, May 29 · 5:30pm – 7:30pm · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd Thinking about buying a new tablet, laptop, computer, or smart TV but not sure where to start? This class walks you through what to look for and which tech specs actually matter before making a purchase. Registration required; call 716-773-7124.

8. 🎨 Watercolor Iris Painting Workshop

Saturday, May 30 · 6:00pm · Grand Island Memorial Library, 1715 Bedell Rd Instructor Adele Upton guides participants (ages 18 and up) through an easy watercolor technique to create an iris flower painting suitable for cards, bookmarks, or framing. No prior watercolor experience needed, and all materials are provided. Registration required; call 716-773-7124.
