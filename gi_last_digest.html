1. 🙌 Relay For Life of Grand Island — 25th Anniversary Saturday, Jun 06 · 3:00–10:30 PM · Town Commons, 1959 Whitehaven Road

This year marks the 25th anniversary of Relay For Life on Grand Island, the American Cancer Society's signature fundraiser. The event runs from 3 p.m. to 10:30 p.m. at Town Commons.

The day opens with the Kevin Doring Memorial Walk at 10 a.m., followed by a car/truck/bike show and lawnmower races starting at noon, including a free kids' lawnmower race. Look for face painting, a photo booth, a flag quilt raffle, and Boy Scouts conducting a flag retirement ceremony throughout the afternoon.

More information and team registration is available at relayforlife.org/GrandIslandNY.
